Professional Summary

Medical Doctor with over 8 years of clinical and leadership experience across emergency medicine, internal medicine/ICU, pediatrics, neonatology, and humanitarian field operations. Former Medical Director of a Level 1 clinic for the Rwandan Formed Police Unit (MINUSCA) in Kaga-Bandoro, where he coordinated multidisciplinary teams, oversaw clinic operations, and served as the mission’s COVID-19 focal point. Provided comprehensive medical care to UN personnel, humanitarian workers, local staff, and internally displaced persons. Previously led Emergency, Internal Medicine/ICU, and Pediatrics services at Kibogora Hospital in Rwanda, with a strong focus on quality care, team leadership, and service delivery in resource-limited settings. Currently overseeing daily clinical and administrative operations to ensure efficient, safe, and high-quality patient care at Polyclinic la Medical Huye.
